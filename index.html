Temporal Semantics

Time is on my side, yes it is
– The Rolling Stones

This small website is all about Semantic Web representations of and handling of time.

The reason for this work is that sophisticated handling of time in complex semantic datasets has proved, to this author at least, to be unexpectedly difficult over the last few years, despite there being ontologies and so on dedicated to it.

While temporality may be an aspect of data that is simpler than spatiality, perhaps fewer resources have been dedicated to good handling of it...
